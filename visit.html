Admission

The entrance is free for all. There are guided tours of the museum that leave every hour.

Accessability

The museum has wheelchair accessibility ramps. It also has audio guides and braille display signs for the visually impaired.

We have good discounts with different transportation agencies.

BUS - TRANSPORTATION FOR WHEELCHAIR - TAXI

Please contact us for arrangements

Food & drinks

There is a café attached to the museum where you can get light lunches, soft drinks, coffee, snacks and more.

Shop

Our shop offers a range of memorabilia from the museum as well as great gifts and activity packs
