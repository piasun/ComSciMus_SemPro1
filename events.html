SPECIAL EVENTS

Night in the museum

Get your family together for an exciting night in the museum as you sleep over beside dinosaurs and science displays. Bring your own sleeping bag and get ready to rough it as we go exploring the wonders of science.

Visiting Professor of Aeronautics

It is our pleasure to announce that Prof Sheila Widnall from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology will be delivering 3 lectures on the development of aeronautics and where the future lies in this exciting ‘space’.

Energetica Exhibition on Loan

On loan from the NEMO Science Museum in Amsterdam, the Energetica exhibition is coming to the Community Science Museum. It’s a series of installations that allow visitors to experience the power of the elements as we harness them.

WEBINARS

Science Show

Science show with surprising tricks with soap bubbles, chemistry and liquid nitrogen! See flames, smoke, witch hazel and volcanic bubbles!

Electricity Show

Did you know that light is affected by magnets? We show a hostage pipe and a terrella in the exhibition at the museum. Here you can see how light is affected by magnets and magnetic fields.

Astronomy Show

Is there life on other planets? What will happen to our sun? How does a space rocket work? These are some of the questions you will get answered in our new astronomy show. Using experiments and simulations, we seek answers to the universe's biggest secrets!

KIDS CORNER

Are you a young person looking to learn more about science? Come on down to our museum, there’s plenty to see and do. You can learn about Newtonian physics from our bumper swing, or why not travel back in time and meet our resident Woolly Mammoth?

Our exhibits are designed to be accessible for interested minds, so make sure you come ready to learn and explore. We also have the Young Stars club which meets once a week on a Saturday between 10:00 and 13:00 where you’ll get to explore and experiment with our team of experts.

During the school holidays we run special holiday clubs where you can join other children your age to go on a journey of discovery. Each holiday we pick a new theme to explore. To find out more about the holiday club and how you can join, send us a message.
